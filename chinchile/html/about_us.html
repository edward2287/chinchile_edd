Story

For more than 10 years Magic Clean have been manufacturing and distributing supplies in the area of Miami, Florida under the name of Magic Clean Products. We offer a variety of products for different industries such as janitorial, automotive, food industry, laundry, among others.

Products

We manufacture products that are formulated to bring maximum cleaning potential to commercial applications, including commercial kitchens, floors, bathrooms, textiles, laundry, cars, trucks, RVs, boats and more. Our products are perfect for car wash companies, truck parts stores, boats cleaners, hospitals, schools, and companies specializing in commercial and residential cleaning.
